📌 How to Use
Click "Choose File" to upload a JPEG or PNG image from your computer.

Select an upscaling method that suits your needs:

Bilinear – Smooth and standard results

Bicubic – Sharper, higher-quality details

Nearest Neighbor – Maintains pixel sharpness (ideal for line art)

Adjust the scaling factor using the slider (from 1.5× to 4×).

Click "Upscale Image" to start the process.

Once completed, click Download to save the result.

✨ How to Use the Clarification Feature
Upload your blurry image as usual.

Choose the "Clarification" method from the available options.

Adjust the three sliders as needed:

Sharpness:

For very blurry images: 70–90%

Noise Reduction:

For images with fine detail: 10–20%

Contrast:

For dark or faded images: 20–40%

Click "Upscale Image" to process.

Download the clarified result.

🛠️ Application Features
Supports JPEG and PNG image uploads

Offers 3 upscaling methods:

🔹 Bilinear – Smooth standard results

🔸 Bicubic – Enhanced sharpness and detail

⚫ Nearest Neighbor – Preserves pixel edges

Choose scaling factor: 1.5× to 4×

Displays image dimensions before and after upscaling

Final result downloadable in PNG format
